Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

Do’s
1. Use strong and secure passwords.
2. Keep your profile professional.
3. Verify information before sharing.
4. Respect others while communicating online.
5. Use privacy settings to protect personal data.

Don’ts
1. Do not share personal or sensitive information.
2. Do not click suspicious links.
3. Avoid posting offensive or harmful content.
4. Do not spread fake news or misinformation.
5. Avoid downloading files from untrusted sources.
